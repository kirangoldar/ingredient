Delicious Recipes

1. Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 500g spaghetti
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.

2. Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a wok or large skillet, heat sesame oil over high heat.
  2. Add chicken and garlic, and stir-fry until the chicken is cooked through.
  3. Add the bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Pour the sauce into the skillet and stir-fry for an additional minute.
  5. Serve the stir-fry over rice or noodles.

3. Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange tomato slices and mozzarella slices on a serving plate, alternating them.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving.

4. Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork.
  3. Add melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture and stir until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

5. Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to the temperature specified on the pizza dough package.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven according to the package instructions, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
